Free MMA Gym - Cathedral City

Free MMA all ages Gym!
*SERIOUSLY ITS FREE* Martial should not be a privilege.
Learn to protect yourself, be strong, be brave and confident! JOIN US!


Free MMA is a free mixed martial arts gym that allows underprivileged youth, low-income adults and seniors to take advantage of the self-discipline and self-defense offered by martial arts training.
Jarred “Bear” Fiorda founded Free MMA in order to give financially insecure households the same opportunities that his mother struggled so hard to provide for him. As an only child being raised by a (widowed) single mother in Desert Hot Springs, California, Jarred has seen first hand the positive effects that martial arts can have on a community, as well as the devastating effect it’s absence can have on its youth. The goal of Free MMA is to make martial arts lessons attainable to all.
Jarred is a 2nd - degree black belt (Taekwondo), a mixed martial artist, instructor of Taekwondo, jujitsu and Hapkido and a competitive MMA cage fighter. Free
